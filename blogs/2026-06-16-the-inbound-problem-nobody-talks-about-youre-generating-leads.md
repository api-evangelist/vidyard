This piece examines why marketing teams successfully attract prospects but fail to convert them, noting that interest stalls in the handoff that is too slow, too generic, and too easy for a prospect to ignore. It presents four critical moments in the inbound funnel where personalized video responses can dramatically improve conversion rates, from form submissions to post-webinar follow-ups.
